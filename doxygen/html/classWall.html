void Wall::moveIfPossible ( sf::Vector2f  direction)
overridevirtual

Move the wall in a specific direction, if it's possible.

The wall gets moved, if and only if the location it moves to does NOT contain any other IObjects. The direction gets multiplied by the 'speed' member. If you move the wall 5 in the X direction, the wall moves 5*speed in the X direction.
